Research Areas in AI for Public Health

Explore impactful domains where AI is transforming population health

Disease Surveillance & Early Warning

AI models that detect and forecast outbreaks (e.g., dengue, COVID-19) from health, mobility, or social media data.

Epidemiology Time Series Outbreak Detection

Epidemiological Modeling

Predict disease spread and burden using AI-powered simulations and population-level health data.

Simulation Predictive Modeling Public Health

Chronic Disease Risk Prediction

Forecast individual risk for diabetes, cardiovascular diseases, or cancer using lifestyle and EHR data.

EHR Risk Scoring ML/DL Models

Digital Behavior & Mental Health

Use AI to analyze social media and wearable data to track mental health, vaccine hesitancy, or smoking behavior.

NLP Social Media Mental Health

NLP for Health Records

Extract insights from clinical notes, reports, or literature using natural language processing (NLP).

NLP Clinical Text Information Extraction

Maternal & Child Health AI

Use AI tools to predict pregnancy risks and improve neonatal outcomes through mobile and community health data.

mHealth Pregnancy Neonatal Care

AI for TB & Disease Detection

Leverage AI on chest X-rays and clinical data to enhance tuberculosis and disease screening in low-resource settings.

Medical Image Analysis CAD4TB Screening

Geospatial AI & Health Equity

Map disease burdens, healthcare access, and inequity using satellite imagery, census, and health data.

GIS Inequality Remote Sensing

AI Decision Support for Policy

Develop AI tools that support public health interventions and decision-making for planners and policymakers.

Dashboards Policy Data-Driven Decisions
